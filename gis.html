Yavapai County Gold Mine Museum Recommendations

For my GIS class final project, I had selected the business decision support option where the Arizona Department of Tourism is seeking to build a museum that honors the rich history of gold mining in Yavapai County, Arizona. I had chosen this topic because I am interested in the tourism industry and was in Yavapai County in December 2020 for vacation. For this project, I provided a map of site recommendations that depicts all eligible land and used ArcGIS to find three potential sites based upon historical and logistical requests. Since the requests includes site recommendations within one mile of a historic or active gold mine and within three miles of a large retail store, I used range rings to quickly locate potential sites. By sorting land ownership by color, I was able to see the difference between federal land, private land, and American Indian reservation land. The last requirement I looked at was making sure the site recommendation is within 500 feet of a roadway.
